# Contributor Covenant Code of Conduct — DiLang

## 1. Our Pledge
We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in the DiLang project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, visible or invisible disability, ethnicity, sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience, education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## 2. Our Standards
- Use welcoming and inclusive language.
- Be respectful of differing opinions and architectural viewpoints.
- Focus on technical excellence, modularity, and deterministic learning algorithms.
- Gracefully accept constructive criticism.

## 3. Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported to the project lead team. All complaints will be reviewed and investigated promptly and fairly.
