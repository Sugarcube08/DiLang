## 1. Brand Identity Overview

DiLang's brand identity represents a premium, intelligent, offline-first language learning experience.

- **Symbol**: Neural Language Nodes (interconnected linguistic graph).
- **Wordmark**: DiLang (Clean, sans-serif variable geometry).
- **Safe Area**: Minimum 16dp margins around logo mark.
- **Grids & Spacing**: 8-point spatial grid system (8dp, 16dp, 24dp, 32dp, 48dp).
