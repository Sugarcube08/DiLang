## 1. Gamification Replacement

DiLang replaces arbitrary XP points, streaks, and badges with an **Evidence-Based CEFR Progression Model**.

CEFR Level promotion (e.g. A1 $\rightarrow$ A2) requires demonstrating empirical competence across four core criteria:

1. **Vocabulary Coverage**: 85%+ mastery of required A1 lexical items ($S \ge 30$ days).
2. **Grammar Competence**: Zero structural errors in 10 consecutive main-clause compositions.
3. **Pronunciation IPA Accuracy**: Acoustic score $\ge 0.85$ across target language phonemes.
4. **Conversation Transfer**: Successful completion of 5 unassisted dialogue scenarios.
