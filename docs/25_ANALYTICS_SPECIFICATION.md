## 1. Event-Sourced Analytics

Analytics metrics are computed asynchronously by listening to domain event streams, guaranteeing zero UI overhead.

- **Retention Curves**: Plotting predicted retrievability decay $R(t)$ against empirical recall test results.
- **Daily Learning Efficiency**: Ratio of successful reviews to total reviews per 24-hour period.
- **Vocabulary Growth Rate**: Net active vocabulary expansion per week.
- **Time-to-Mastery**: Average hours required to progress an item from `new_item` to `mastered`.
