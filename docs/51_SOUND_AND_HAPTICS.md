## 1. Tactile & Audio Feedback System

Audio cues:
- `sound_success`: Crisp, soft confirmation chime.
- `sound_review_correct`: FSRS retention recall feedback chime.
- `sound_conversation_ready`: AI dialogue initialized tone.

Haptics:
- `haptic_tap`: Light impact (`10ms`).
- `haptic_correct`: Double pulse success.
- `haptic_error`: Heavy warning pulse.
